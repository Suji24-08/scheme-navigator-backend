SCHEME NAME: Pragati Scholarship Scheme for Girls
ADMINISTERING BODY: All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
SCHEME TYPE: Central Sector Scheme

OBJECTIVE: Encourage and financially support girl students pursuing technical education (diploma/degree) to improve gender participation in technical fields.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must be a female student and Indian citizen
- Must be admitted to the 1st year of a diploma or degree technical course, OR 2nd year via lateral entry, at an AICTE-approved institution
- Annual family income (both parents combined) must not exceed Rs. 8,00,000
- Only two girl children per family are eligible under the scheme
- Must not have received the scholarship previously for the same course (first-year/lateral-entry admission only)

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Rs. 50,000 per year (lump sum) for tuition fees, books, equipment, laptop/software purchase; disbursed for up to 3 years (degree, first-year admission) or up to 2 years (lateral entry)

TOTAL SLOTS: 10,000 scholarships annually (5,000 for degree courses, 5,000 for diploma courses)

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Continued enrollment and satisfactory academic progress in the same AICTE-approved institution

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), applications typically close around October 31 each year

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Class 10/12 marksheets, income certificate, admission letter, institute certificate, fee receipt, bank passbook, parental declaration

SOURCE: AICTE / National Scholarship Portal guidelines, verified June 2026
