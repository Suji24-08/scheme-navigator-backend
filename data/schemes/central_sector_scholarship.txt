SCHEME NAME: Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship (CSSS) for College and University Students / PM-USP
ADMINISTERING BODY: Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India
SCHEME TYPE: 100% Centrally Funded Scheme
LAUNCHED: 2008

OBJECTIVE: Merit-cum-means scholarship to support meritorious students from economically weaker sections in pursuing undergraduate/postgraduate higher education.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Must score above the 80th percentile among successful candidates in the relevant stream in Class 12 board examination (CBSE/State Board/ICSE)
- Must be pursuing a REGULAR degree course (correspondence, distance education, and diploma courses are NOT eligible)
- Must be enrolled at a college/institution recognized by AICTE/UGC or other relevant regulatory body, with valid AISHE code
- Annual gross parental/family income must not exceed Rs. 4,50,000
- Must not be availing any other scholarship, fee waiver, or reimbursement scheme (Central or State)
- Age limit: 18-25 years
- Reservation: SC 15%, ST 7.5%, OBC 27%, PwD 5%; 50% of total scholarships reserved for girls

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Rs. 10,000/year for years 1-3 of undergraduate study; Rs. 20,000/year for postgraduate study or years 4-5 of integrated/professional courses (e.g., B.Tech/B.E.)

DURATION: Maximum 5 years, covering graduation and post-graduation in the same stream

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Minimum 50% marks in previous year's exam and minimum 75% attendance

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), renewal deadline typically October 31

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Class 12 marksheet, income certificate (fresh applicants only), caste certificate (if applicable), Aadhaar, bank passbook

SOURCE: Ministry of Education / National Scholarship Portal guidelines, verified June 2026
