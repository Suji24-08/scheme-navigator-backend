SCHEME NAME: PM-YASASVI (Pradhan Mantri Young Achievers Scholarship Award Scheme for Vibrant India) — Top Class Education component
ADMINISTERING BODY: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India
SCHEME TYPE: Centrally Sponsored Scheme

OBJECTIVE: Provide financial support for higher/technical education to students from Other Backward Classes (OBC), Economically Backward Classes (EBC), and Denotified/Nomadic/Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNT).

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must belong to OBC, EBC, or DNT category with a valid caste certificate
- Annual family/parental income must not exceed Rs. 2,50,000
- Must be currently enrolled in Class 9 or Class 11 at a government-recognized school (for the school-level component), OR admitted to a full-time course at a notified institution with valid UDISE/AISHE code (for higher education component)
- Selection is merit-based, using previous class board exam marks (entrance test discontinued as of 2026 cycle)
- No more than two siblings from the same family can benefit from the scheme simultaneously
- Applicant must be an Indian citizen

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Up to Rs. 75,000/year for Class 9-10 students; up to Rs. 1,25,000/year for Class 11-12 students; for higher education, covers tuition fees, living allowance (~Rs. 3,000/month), books allowance (~Rs. 5,000/year), and one-time laptop allowance (~Rs. 45,000)

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Must maintain satisfactory academic performance and minimum 75% attendance; must apply for renewal annually via NSP

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), applications typically close around August 31 each year

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Aadhaar card, caste certificate (OBC/EBC/DNT), income certificate, Class 8 or 10 marksheet, school/UDISE verification, bank passbook

SOURCE: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment / National Scholarship Portal guidelines, verified June 2026
