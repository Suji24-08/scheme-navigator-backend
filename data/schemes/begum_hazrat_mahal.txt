SCHEME NAME: Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship (formerly Maulana Azad Scholarship for Girls)
ADMINISTERING BODY: Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF), Ministry of Minority Affairs
SCHEME TYPE: National-level scholarship, girls-only

OBJECTIVE: Financially support meritorious girl students from minority communities to complete schooling without financial hardship.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must be a GIRL student (this scheme is exclusively for female students)
- Must belong to a notified minority community: Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, or Parsi
- Must be studying in Class 9, 10, 11, or 12
- Must have secured at least 50% marks or equivalent grade in the previous class
- Annual family income from all sources must not exceed Rs. 2,00,000
- Not applicable for studies abroad

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Rs. 5,000/year for Class 9-10; Rs. 6,000/year for Class 11-12 (some sources cite up to Rs. 10,000-12,000 depending on component — verify current year rate on application)

APPLICATION: Maulana Azad Education Foundation portal / National Scholarship Portal, typically open September-October

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Income certificate, minority community self-declaration, school verification form, previous class marksheet, Aadhaar, bank details

NOTE FOR SYSTEM: This scheme does not extend beyond Class 12 — if a matched user is already in college, this scheme should NOT be suggested; instead point toward Post-Matric Minority Scholarship or Pragati (if eligible).

SOURCE: Maulana Azad Education Foundation / Ministry of Minority Affairs guidelines, verified June 2026
