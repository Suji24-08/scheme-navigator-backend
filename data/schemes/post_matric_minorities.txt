SCHEME NAME: Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minorities
ADMINISTERING BODY: Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India
SCHEME TYPE: Centrally Sponsored Scheme

OBJECTIVE: Provide financial assistance to meritorious students from economically weaker sections of minority communities to increase their attainment in higher education and enhance employability.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must belong to a notified minority community: Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, or Parsi (Zoroastrian)
- Must be enrolled in Class 11, 12, or post-matric courses (undergraduate, postgraduate, technical, vocational, ITI, polytechnic)
- Must have secured at least 50% marks in the previous qualifying examination
- Annual family income from all sources must not exceed Rs. 2,00,000
- Maximum of two students per family are eligible
- Students in correspondence or distance learning programs are NOT eligible
- Must not be availing benefits from any other central or state scholarship scheme

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Course fee reimbursement up to Rs. 10,000/year plus maintenance allowance up to Rs. 1,200/month (paid for 10 months), varies by state and course level

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Minimum 50% marks in previous year's examination

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in) under Ministry of Minority Affairs, deadlines typically in late October

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Minority community self-certification, income certificate, previous year's marksheet, Aadhaar, bank passbook

SOURCE: Ministry of Minority Affairs / National Scholarship Portal guidelines, verified June 2026
