SCHEME NAME: Top Class Education Scheme for Scheduled Caste Students (Central Sector Scholarship)
ADMINISTERING BODY: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India
SCHEME TYPE: Central Sector Scheme
LAUNCHED: 2007

OBJECTIVE: Promote quality higher education among SC students by providing full financial support for admission to premier institutions.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must belong to Scheduled Caste category
- Must have secured admission to a full-time UG/PG course at a notified premier institution (IITs, IIMs, NITs, IIITs, AIIMS, National Law Universities, NIFTs, NIDs, Institutes of Hotel Management, NAAC A+/A++ accredited institutions, or top-100 NIRF-ranked institutions)
- Annual family income from all sources must not exceed Rs. 8,00,000
- Fresh scholarship available only to first-year students
- Maximum of two siblings per family can benefit under this scheme
- 30% of slots per institution reserved for SC girl students (transferable to boys if insufficient female applicants)

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Full tuition fee and non-refundable charges (capped at Rs. 2,00,000/year for private institutions, Rs. 3,72,000/year for private commercial pilot training); academic/maintenance allowance of approximately Rs. 86,000 in first year and Rs. 41,000/year thereafter

TOTAL SLOTS: Approximately 1,250 fresh scholarships annually

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Scholarship terminated if student fails final exam of any year; resumes upon passing and promotion. Discontinued if student changes institution.

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), applications typically open June 1, close October 31

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: SC caste certificate, income certificate (issued by Revenue Officer not below Tehsildar rank), Aadhaar, admission letter, fee receipt, sibling declaration affidavit

SOURCE: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment guidelines, verified June 2026
