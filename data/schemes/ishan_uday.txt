SCHEME NAME: Ishan Uday - Special Scholarship Scheme for North Eastern Region (NER)
ADMINISTERING BODY: University Grants Commission (UGC)
SCHEME TYPE: Centrally Funded Scheme (region-specific)
LAUNCHED: 2014-15

OBJECTIVE: Increase Gross Enrolment Ratio in higher education among economically weaker students from India's North Eastern states.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Applicant must be domiciled in one of the North Eastern states: Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura (Sikkim sometimes included — verify current state list)
- Must have passed Class 12 (or equivalent) in the preceding year, from a school within NER
- Must be enrolled in the FIRST YEAR of a regular, full-time UG degree program (general, technical, medical, or paramedical)
- Annual family income must not exceed Rs. 4,50,000
- Students in Open University, correspondence, distance, or management-quota admissions are NOT eligible
- Diploma or non-degree courses are NOT covered
- Transgender applicants are explicitly eligible

BENEFIT AMOUNT: Rs. 5,400/month for general degree courses; Rs. 7,800/month for technical/medical/professional/paramedical courses (paid for 10 months/year), disbursed via DBT

TOTAL SLOTS: 10,000 fresh scholarships annually, distributed among NER states by population

RENEWAL CONDITIONS: Must reapply annually; change of course or institution is not permitted without forfeiting the scholarship

APPLICATION: National Scholarship Portal (scholarships.gov.in), typically closes around January 15 (verify current-year deadline)

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS: Domicile certificate, Class 10 and 12 marksheets, income certificate, Aadhaar, bank details, caste certificate if applicable

SOURCE: University Grants Commission guidelines, verified June 2026
